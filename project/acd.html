Academics

Eligibility: 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.

Master of Business Administration (MBA)

Duration: 2 years

Specializations: Marketing, Finance, Human Resources, Operations, and IT Management.

Eligibility: Graduation in any discipline with a valid entrance exam score.

Highlights: Case-based learning, global immersion programs, and placement assistance.

Duration: 3 years

Eligibility: 10+2 with relevant subjects.

Highlights: Research opportunities, modern labs, and collaborations with research institutions.

Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D)
