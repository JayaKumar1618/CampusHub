History of SRM University

SRM University, SRM University (Sri Ramaswamy Memorial University) was established in 1985 as SRM Engineering College in Kattankulathur, Tamil Nadu. Over the years, it evolved into one of India’s premier private universities, and doctoral programs across diverse disciplines like engineering, medicine, management, and the arts. With a strong emphasis on research, innovation, and global exposure, SRM has collaborations with numerous international universities and attracts students from all over the world.

The motto of SRM University is "Learn, Leap, Lead", reflecting its commitment to empowering students with knowledge, encouraging them to think beyond boundaries, and preparing them to be leaders in their chosen fields. The institution aims to foster academic excellence, ensuring that students are well-equipped to face global challenges and make meaningful contributions to society..
